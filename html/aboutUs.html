ABOUT US

My name is Ricardo Casillas. I was born in Guadalajara, Jalisco. At the age of 7 I moved to Olivehurst, California with my grandfather where my love for boxing began. As a kid boxing was a way to bring our family together at the living room of grandfather's house. In my teenage years I moved from place to place from one school to another and never really knowing where we would go next. I remember walking into my first boxing gym located in Oakland, California. This is were I discovered that boxing is not just a sport. Boxing is way of living. A way of relaxation,discipline, stress reliever. It builds self control and confidence. Now as a Coach, I want to help kids and adults in and around my community feel confident in themselves mentally and physically. At Slick Rick Boxing we are more than just a boxing gym we are a family here to help improve your skills in boxing and take you to the next level.
